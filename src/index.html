FCancer is dedicated to the prevention and early detection of cancer, it is a movement that creates a raw and authentic space by harnessing the cumulative experience of their community, Generation Connected. Through digital tools, programs and events, FCancer believes its community will be the generation that ultimately improves health outcomes. FCancer is a tax-exempt 501(c) 3 registered charity co-founded by Yael Cohen Braun and Julie Greenbaun.
